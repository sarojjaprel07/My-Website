About Story

A story is a way to share experiences, ideas, and emotions with others. It can be told through words, pictures, or actions. Stories help us understand the world around us by showing events that happen to people or characters. They often have a beginning, middle, and end, which keeps the listener or reader interested.

Stories can teach us lessons, entertain us, or make us think deeply. From ancient times, people have used stories to pass knowledge from one generation to another. Whether it is a fairy tale, a personal experience, or a fictional adventure, stories connect us and make us feel less alone.
