About Me

A Journey of Understanding

I'm Saroj Japrel.

I know that, in reality, I know very little. The universe is infinite, and the world is full of mysteries I can never fully understand — and that's okay. That's why I'm here: to keep learning, to explore little by little, and to slowly understand not only the world around me but also myself.

I'm simply trying to stay curious — to keep an open heart and a questioning mind. I believe we grow step by step, not all at once, and it's okay not to have all the answers right away.

Through this small space, I share parts of that journey — what I feel, what I learn, and the moments that make me stop and wonder. It's not about being perfect or knowing everything; it's about staying curious and enjoying the process of learning.

Stay curious. Stay humble. And let life teach you in its own quiet way.
