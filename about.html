About Me

I’m Saroj Japrel.

I know that, in reality, I don’t know much — and maybe I’ll never know everything. The world is huge and full of things I can’t fully understand, but that’s okay. That’s why I’m here — to keep learning, to explore little by little, and to slowly understand not just the world around me, but also myself. I’m just trying to stay curious — to keep an open heart and a mind that’s always asking questions. I believe we grow step by step, not all at once, and that it’s okay to not have all the answers right away. Through this little space, I share pieces of that journey — what I feel, what I learn, and the things that make me stop and wonder. It’s not about being perfect or knowing everything; it’s about staying curious and enjoying the process of learning.

"Stay curious. Stay humble. And let life teach you in its own quiet way."
